Project Overview

During last year's VBS 2025, My co-worker and I were tasked with creating media plans for our yearly VBS Summer "Camp" week. This involved creating engaging media showing what the kids were up to the whole week!

Key areas of focus included making advertisements for the event, while also making daily recap picture posts on social media showing what each group was up to each night. By the end of the week, we were tasked with planning and creating a Recap Video for the whole event to show during the following Sunday Church Worship!
