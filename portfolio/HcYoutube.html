Holy Cross YouTube Media Campaign

Revitalizing the digital presence through consistent branding and engaging thumbnails.

Project Overview

I undertook a complete redesign of the Holy Cross YouTube channel to improve viewer engagement and brand consistency. The goal was to create a modern, welcoming aesthetic that aligns with the church's values while making content easier to find and identify.

Key areas of focus included creating recognizable and consistent thumbnails for livestreams, creating high quality sermon videos, creating quality sermon shorts based off of the weeks sermon, and refreshing the channel banner to provide a cohesive visual identity.

One major want for this Media Campaign overhaul was to create an engaging, informational video encapsulating different growth opportunities for Holy Cross and encouraging new and current members to get connected with lifegroups and furthering their faith in Christ.

  • Filming worship services for B-Roll
  • Filming youth group B-Roll & Lifegroups
  • Editing / Combining audio and video clips
  • Creating and animating lower third graphics for more information
  • Revising and editing the video based on staff feedback
